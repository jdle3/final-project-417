Members

Yeji, born on May 26, 2000, is the charismatic leader of ITZY. Known for her powerful stage presence and versatile talents, Yeji has become a standout figure in the K-pop scene. Beyond her skills as a rapper and dancer, she possesses a captivating charm that resonates with fans around the world.
Lia, born on July 21, 2000, is the lead vocalist of ITZY, recognized for her enchanting voice and graceful demeanor. Her soulful vocals add depth to the group's music, while her elegant presence on stage has earned her a dedicated fanbase. Lia's ability to convey emotion through her singing is a defining aspect of ITZY's sound.
Ryujin, born on April 17, 2001, is ITZY's main rapper and lead dancer. Her powerful rap delivery and dynamic dance moves contribute to the group's dynamic performances. With her confident and cool image, Ryujin has become a trendsetter, inspiring fans with her fashion sense and unapologetic attitude.
Chaeryeong, born on June 5, 2001, serves as ITZY's main dancer and sub-vocalist. Recognized for her exceptional dance skills and versatility, Chaeryeong brings a dynamic energy to the group's choreography. Her ability to seamlessly switch between powerful and graceful movements adds a unique dimension to ITZY's stage presence.
Yuna, born on December 9, 2003, is ITZY's youngest member and sub-vocalist. Despite her young age, Yuna has made a significant impact with her sweet vocals and charming personality. Her stage presence, combined with her vibrant energy, completes the synergy within ITZY, making the group a force to be reckoned with in the K-pop industry.
